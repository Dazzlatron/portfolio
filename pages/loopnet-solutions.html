Project Overview:

LoopNet is CoStar Group’s leading commercial property marketplace, used by 96% of Fortune 1000 firms. Last year, it drove over $380B in transactions—representing more than 90% of all commercial real estate deals. When LoopNet launched in the UK, I was tasked with building the marketing website for the UK market.

This project required me to get acquainted with a Drupal-based content management tool called Site Studio, and to build a drag-and-drop UI component library to make website maintenance easier for people who don’t code.
