ABOUT ME

Daryn Higginson.

London, United Kingdom

With over a decade of experience in digital design and web development across marketing agencies and in-house teams, I've built a strong foundation in visual design and technical expertise. More recently, I transitioned into a UI/UX developer role, which allows me to combine my love for creating, problem-solving and technology.

I leverage research and data to make informed decisions that enhance user experiences and I love to collaborate and create solutions that are both functional and engaging. I also really enjoy learning new trends, technologies and music production.

In my spare time you can find me cooking, making music, cycling, gardening, reading, watching movies, and playing video games.

MY PHILOSOPHY

Where Design and Function Meet.

Creativity

I put creativity at the heart of everything I do. I use it to solve problems, develop intuitive experiences, and create engaging designs. Creativity made the music I listen to while I work—it drives and inspires me. What would the world be like without music, art, and expression?

Technology

I use technology to bring ideas to life, communicate, and interact in ways we never thought possible. With technology I can offer better solutions and create better, more fulfilling experiences. Technology gave me the headphones I listen to music on while I work.

Magic

I believe that the combination of these two seemingly opposite forces can drive innovation, spark meaningful experiences, and solve real-world problems. This is why I keep honing my creativity and expanding my technical know-how. Curiosity is key.
